# Slash Commercial Licensing

Slash is built with a dual commercialization approach. While the desktop client and local editor packages are open-source under the MIT license, remote synchronization services and commercial hosting of team features are subject to commercial licensing.

## Personal & Small Teams Use

- **Free Personal Use**: You can use the Slash Desktop client and the local runtime completely free for personal use.
- **Small Teams (Up to 5 members)**: Small team sync configurations using official beta channels are free during the beta phase.

## Commercial & Enterprise Use

For larger deployments, corporate sync infrastructure, and managed team hosting:
- **Hosted Cloud Sync**: A subscription fee applies to official cloud synchronization hosting, database maintenance, and live collaboration seats.
- **Enterprise On-Premises**: If your organization requires on-premises deployment of `slash-server` with active directory integration (LDAP/SAML), advanced permission compliance, and premium support, a commercial enterprise license is required.

For licensing inquiries or to request beta-partner pricing, please contact us at `licensing@slash.tools`.
